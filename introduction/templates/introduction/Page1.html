Dear Participant,
We are a group of researchers from Nazarbayev University studying investment decision making and its behavioral aspects. The research intends to study how individuals construct their investment portfolios in the dynamic setup. Before proceeding with the survey, we strongly recommend you familiarize yourself with the information presented below.

Procedures: To perform the research, we are conducting an online experiment. In order to participate in the experiment, you should complete all tasks presented in this web interface. Your participation is voluntary and you can withdraw at any time. This experiment is confidential and takes approximately 40-80 minutes to complete.

Risks: No significant risks for experiment participants have been identified during the elaboration of the tasks.

Benefits: By conducting this research we want to contribute to the general knowledge of behavioral finance. The findings of this study will be useful for policymakers who are interested in designing decision support tools and for investment advisors creating investment products that cater to the needs of different types of investors.

Compensation: By participating in the survey you may win a new iPhone. One participant-winner will be chosen by random generator. Please provide your NU ID to participate in the lottery. Additionally, if you wish, we can provide you with a copy of the research results that will be available after the completion of the study.

Points of Contact: In case of any questions and/or comments regarding this project and/or research-related inquiries, you may contact the researchers:
