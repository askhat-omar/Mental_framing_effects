Dear Participant,
We are a group of researchers from Nazarbayev University studying investment decision-making and its behavioral aspects. The research intends to study how individuals construct their investment portfolios in a dynamic setup. Before proceeding with the survey, we strongly recommend you familiarize yourself with the information below.

Procedures: To perform the research, we are conducting an online experiment. In order to participate in the experiment, you should complete all tasks presented in this web interface. Your participation is voluntary, and you can withdraw at any time. This experiment is confidential and takes approximately 40-80 minutes to complete.

Risks: No significant risks for experiment participants have been identified during the elaboration of the tasks.

Benefits: By conducting this research, we want to contribute to the general knowledge of behavioral finance. The findings of this study will be helpful for policymakers interested in designing decision support tools and investment advisors creating investment products that cater to the needs of different types of investors.

Compensation: All participants of our experiment will get additional 1% to the total grade of BUS 101 course. Moreover, among the participants, we will randomly choose 5 participants to win money prizes (money amounts depend on points collected during the experiment, typical amounts lie in the range between 10,000 KZT and 50,000 KZT). Please provide your NU ID to participate in the lottery. Additionally, we can provide you with a copy of the research results that will be available after the completion of the study.

Points of Contact: In case of any questions and/or comments regarding this project and/or research-related inquiries, you may contact the researchers:

Any other questions or concerns may be addressed to the Nazarbayev University Institutional Research Ethics Committee, resethics@nu.edu.kz.
